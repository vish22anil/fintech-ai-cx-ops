# AI Impact on Financial Services, Customer Experience & Operational Efficiency in FinTech Firms

This repository contains code, datasets, analysis workflows, and documentation for our IEEE research paper on the impact of Artificial Intelligence in FinTech.

## Project Overview
We explore how AI techniques such as sentiment analysis, process mining, and automation simulation affect:
- Customer Experience (CX)
- Operational Efficiency
- Service Quality Improvements in FinTech companies

## Folder Structure
- **data/** — datasets (raw and processed)
- **notebooks/** — Google Colab notebooks for NLP, process mining, and automation
- **paper/** — IEEE template and paper drafts
- **figures/** — charts and visualizations
- **references/** — research papers and citations
